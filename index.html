logo, re-branding and website concept
Ecadeiras is the biggest e-commerce in the office and design chairs market in Brazil.
  • Re-branding and new logo representing the new stage of the company becoming the biggest e-commerce in this field in Brazil.
  • Re-design and front-end development of the website based on previous users’ navigation, transforming it into a single experience through devices.
  • Data hierarchy re-structuration based on competitors and marketing strategy.
Alcaçuz is a photography company specialized in family and children portraits.
  • The logo reflects the identity and highlights of the company, which are, amongst others, love, professionalism and providing a good environment for the little ones.
  • The branding was made thinking about the goals of the company to become a reference in family and children photography, targeting A and B consumers.
  • Layout and front-end development through the Wordpress platform to simplify the changes made to the catalog and the photographs, which are the foundation of the website.
Menpath is a project of graduate students in fashion design majors to assist men finding the best stores for them based on their body dimensions, lifestyle and location.
  • The Logo reflects the main idea of the project, which is being an interactive guide to all men when it comes to fashion.
  • Branding created to instigate men to get closer to fashion, creating a familiar environment to insert a new point of view to fashion.
  • Layout and android app prototype to support a fashion graduation thesis with the interest of proving the viability and usability of the service/app in the Brazilian market.
Hidrofluxo is one of my graduation group’s projects, based on data visualization regarding the residential water usage.
  • Academic research on sustainability, water resources in Brazil and residential water expenditure in São Paulo.
  • Android app that reveals based on the data received from Arduino, its own water expense in real time, creating graphs and infographics, with the possibility of performing leak tests.
  • Prototype device that, when inserted into the house main water pipe, captures the flow of water at all times, sending, via Wi-Fi, these data to the smartphone.
Awapé is one of my graduation group’s projects and its goal is to be an Invisible Water Consumption Plan, helping users to consume less water.
  • Academic research on sustainability, water footprint, invisible water and water resources in Brazil.
  • The Awapé app is a full-working consumer wizard that aims to raise awareness about the consumption of virtual water present in food and beverages consumed daily.
  • The Awapé collaborative portal is meant to be a reference in the research and information sharing about the theme and in engaging people around it.
Whiplash.net is a website devoted to bring every important news about rock n' roll and metal to the Brazilian market.
  • The Logo values the name and theme of the website, and was updated to a flat-style logo without losing its essence.
  • Re-branding to update the identity that hasn't been changed since the site was created, in 1996. Bringing a fresh start to this has already increased ratings as a news source.
  • The layout is completely changed from my own experience as an user. It shows all the important news and media right away. The search for articles is quicker, due to the fact it has several categories.
